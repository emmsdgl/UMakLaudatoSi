# Laudato Si' Campus Growth Initiative
### A Gamified Sustainability Platform for the University of Makati

---

## The Problem

Universities worldwide are called to act on climate change, yet most campus sustainability efforts rely on one-time events, posters, and voluntary pledges with no follow-through. Students disengage quickly because there is no feedback loop, no accountability, and no reward for consistent action.

## The Solution

**Laudato Si'** is a web-based platform that transforms daily environmental action into a campus-wide game. Students earn points for verified eco-pledges, compete on leaderboards, redeem real rewards, and watch a shared 3D plant grow in real-time as the entire campus contributes.

Inspired by Pope Francis's encyclical *Laudato Si': On Care for Our Common Home*, the platform makes sustainability measurable, engaging, and institutionally managed.

## How It Works

| Step | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Discover** | Students take a Carbon Footprint Calculator tailored to Philippine student life |
| **2. Commit** | They choose an Eco-Path (Green Commute, Mindful Eating, Power Saver, Zero Waste, Water Guardian) |
| **3. Act Daily** | Submit photo-verified pledges of real environmental actions |
| **4. Earn & Grow** | Accumulate points through daily streaks; watch the campus 3D plant grow from seed to tree |
| **5. Play** | Solve a daily Eco-Wordle puzzle for bonus seeds |
| **6. Redeem** | Spend points on food, merchandise, vouchers, and experiences via QR-verified rewards |

## Key Features

- **5 Eco-Paths** with 6 actionable commitments each, grounded in UN Sustainable Development Goals
- **Daily Streak System** rewarding consistency (1-5 pts/day, escalating with streaks)
- **3D Plant Visualization** that grows in real-time as the campus collectively contributes
- **Rewards Marketplace** with QR code verification at campus canteens and partners
- **Eco-Wordle** daily word game driving habitual engagement
- **Donation Campaigns** supporting environmental causes via points or GCash
- **Leaderboards** fostering friendly competition across the student body

## Institutional Governance & Security

- **4-tier admin system**: Super Admin, Student Affairs, Finance, and Canteen Admin roles
- **Pledge review workflow**: Admin-verified submissions with photo proof prevent abuse
- **Complete audit logging**: Every admin action tracked with timestamps, IP addresses, and before/after values
- **Google OAuth authentication**: No passwords to manage; institutional email verification
- **Row-Level Security**: Enterprise-grade data protection on all database tables

## Why This Matters for UMak

1. **Laudato Si' Action Platform Alignment** -- Positions UMak as a leading Catholic university responding to the Vatican's call for ecological conversion
2. **Accreditation & Reporting** -- Generates measurable sustainability data (daily pledges, carbon footprint baselines, participation rates)
3. **Student Engagement** -- Proven gamification mechanics (streaks, rewards, leaderboards) used by platforms with hundreds of millions of users
4. **Low Operating Cost** -- Built on free-tier cloud infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel); primary cost is the rewards budget, which can be offset by campus partners
5. **Scalable** -- Architecture supports expansion to other campuses, departments, or institutional partners

## Technology

Built with industry-standard tools: **Next.js 14**, **React**, **TypeScript**, **Supabase (PostgreSQL)**, **Three.js** for 3D visualization, and **Google OAuth**. The platform includes **46 API endpoints**, real-time WebSocket updates, and is fully mobile-responsive.

## Proposed Next Step

**Pilot Program**: Deploy to one college or department for one semester. Measure daily active users, pledge completion rates, carbon footprint awareness improvement, and reward redemption volume. Use data to justify campus-wide rollout.
